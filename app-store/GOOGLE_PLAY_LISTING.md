# Google Play Store Listing - StageTimer Remote

## App Name
StageTimer Remote

## Short Description (80 characters max)

## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Control your StageTimer.io countdown timers from anywhere with this powerful native remote control app.

★ PERFECT FOR ★
• Event producers and stage managers
• Conference and meeting organizers
• Church and worship teams
• Broadcast and livestream producers
• Teachers and presenters
• Anyone using StageTimer.io

★ KEY FEATURES ★

► LIVE TIMER CONTROL
Play, pause, stop, and navigate between timers with large, easy-to-tap buttons. Perfect for controlling timers while backstage or across the room.

► REAL-TIME SYNC
See your timer display update in real-time. Changes made anywhere sync instantly across all devices.

► QUICK TIME ADJUSTMENTS
Add or subtract time on the fly - from 30 seconds to 60 minutes - with just two taps.

► CREATE TIMERS ON-THE-GO
Add new countdown timers directly from your phone. Choose from presets or create custom durations.

► HOME SCREEN WIDGET
Control playback without even opening the app. Play, pause, stop, and skip timers right from your home screen.

► EASY SETUP WIZARD
Connect to your StageTimer room in seconds. Our setup wizard automatically detects your Room ID - just log in and go.

► HAPTIC FEEDBACK
Feel every button press with satisfying tactile feedback. Know your commands registered without looking at the screen.

► DARK MODE INTERFACE
Designed for low-light environments like backstage areas, control rooms, and auditoriums.

★ REQUIREMENTS ★
• Active StageTimer.io account with API access
• Internet connection

Note: This is a third-party remote control app for StageTimer.io. A StageTimer.io subscription with API access is required.
