# Privacy Policy for StageTimer Remote

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Overview

StageTimer Remote ("the App") is a remote control application for StageTimer.io. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **StageTimer Credentials:** Your Room ID and API Key are stored locally on your device to connect to your StageTimer.io room. These credentials are never transmitted to our servers.

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect personal information
- We do not collect usage analytics
- We do not collect location data
- We do not use tracking or advertising SDKs
- We do not share any data with third parties

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:
- Room ID and API Key are stored in secure local storage (AsyncStorage/SharedPreferences)
- No data is transmitted to external servers other than StageTimer.io's API

## Third-Party Services

The App communicates only with StageTimer.io's servers to:
- Authenticate your connection using your API key
- Send timer control commands
- Receive real-time timer updates

Please review [StageTimer.io's Privacy Policy](https://stagetimer.io/privacy/) for information about how they handle your data.

## Data Security

- Credentials are stored locally and never leave your device except when authenticating with StageTimer.io
- API keys are transmitted securely over HTTPS
- We recommend keeping your API key private and not sharing it

## Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App Store listing.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:
- GitHub Issues: https://github.com/mattm10101/stagetimer-remote/issues
- Email: [Your email]

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your StageTimer credentials can be cleared at any time by:
1. Opening the App
2. Going to Settings
3. Clearing the Room ID and API Key fields
4. Or uninstalling the App
